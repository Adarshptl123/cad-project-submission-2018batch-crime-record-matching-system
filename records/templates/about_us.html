About Us

We are crime management system, providing computerized management of crime records. Here a admin/officer can insert records such as criminal's profile, investigation reports, FIR's, etc. Police can use this application to manage different crimes and some of the works which is done in police station manually, also use the face matching system for which is provided to match and identify registered offenders for ease of investigation. Officers are registered to the application for its' use. This system helps Police to easily manage documents, complaints and ease their manual work and help in investigations.
